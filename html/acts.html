The Acts of the Apostles

Acts 1:11 - Angels testify that Jesus will come again

"Ye men of Galilee, why stand ye gazing up into heaven? this same Jesus, which is taken up
from you into heaven, shall so come in like manner as ye have seen him go into heaven."

Acts 10:43 - All prophets testify that Jesus is the Christ

"To him give all the prophets witness, that through his name whosoever believeth in him
shall receive remission of sins."

Acts 5:11, 15-16 - The Apostles heal many in Christ's name

"And by the hands of the apostles were many signs and wonders wrought among the people...
Insomuch that they brought forth the sick into the streets, and laid them on beds and couches,
that at the least the shadow of Peter passing by might overshadow some of them. There came
also a multitude out of the cities round about unto Jerusalem, bringing sick folks, and them
which were vexed with unclean spirits: and they were healed every one."
